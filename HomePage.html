About

If you had to name ten things in life a man should know before his is considered a fully rounded adult it’s doubtful the difference between oxfords and brogues would make the cut. However, if you wish to excel at pub quizzes; aspire to the polymathic brilliance of Stephen Fry or indeed seek to be a well-shod man, then a grasp of shoe taxonomy is a higher priority.

Features:

  • Closed lacing, i.e. the faces with the eyelets are sewn under a seam known as the throat.

  • Smooth leather, either plain or patent.

  • Toe cap.

  • Vamp.

  • Plain quarter and no heel cap.
